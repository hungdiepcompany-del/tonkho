## Execution Order

1. preflight project/account.
2. verify collection absent or safe.
3. confirm the owner-approved one known invoice input.
4. derive deterministic jobId.
5. write one SHADOW job.
6. read back the job.
7. attempt one idempotent rerun.
8. verify no duplicate job.
9. verify immutable plan.
10. verify events/reports.
11. verify no external mutations.
12. capture sanitized evidence.
